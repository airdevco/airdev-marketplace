RISKS

Why get a Bubble app audit?

Bubble owners choose a comprehensive audit to identify & prevent the risks that plague apps as they scale.

Data Leaks

80% of Bubble apps have security vulnerabilities; more users means more risk of costly and damaging data leaks.

Latency & Crashes

Poor app design can cause slowdowns, inconsistency, and crashes that are entirely preventable on Bubble.

Cost Spikes

Spikes in "workload units" (WUs) can lead to unexpected surges in Bubble app costs as an app scales.

Technical Debt

Bubble allows for rapid feature development, but also rapid accumulation of technical debt that can stall future progress.

AUDIT SCOPE

What you get from our Bubble app audit

The audit is a comprehensive, step-by-step review of every part of your application.

Database

We evaluate the data structure, privacy rules, option sets, and linkages against the current and potential future needs.

Security

We check for potential vulnerabilities and risks in the application, including exposed data and not secured pages.

Logic

We evaluate the proper use of reusables, search logic, as well as clear and concise workflows.

UX

We evaluate the general user experience, including intuitive navigation, feedback to users, responsive design.

Maintenance

We check whether the application has clean and organized workflows and elements for easy maintenance.

Action plan

We create a custom action plan based on what we discover and rank each action item on importance.
